Pork Noodle Casserole

Ingredients

    "2 cups egg noodles", "Cooking spray", "3 tablespoons butter", "¼ cup chopped onion", "¼ cup chopped celery", "¼ cup chopped carrots", "¼ cup chopped red bell pepper", "3 cups cubed cooked pork", "2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup", "2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese", "1 (8 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained", "½ cup sour cream", "1 teaspoon salt", "¼ teaspoon ground black pepper", "½ cup dry bread crumbs (optional)"

Instructions

"Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.", "Cook egg noodles according to package instructions and set aside.", "Melt butter in a skillet, cook onion, celery, carrots, and bell pepper until softened.", "Combine noodles, pork, soup, cheese, corn, sour cream, salt, and pepper. Transfer to baking dish.", "Top with bread crumbs and bake for 30-35 minutes until bubbly."
