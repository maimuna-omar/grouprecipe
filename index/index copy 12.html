Recipe Ingredients

Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients

    "2 onions (peeled and finely chopped)", "6 garlic cloves (crushed)", "3 tbsp brown sugar", "1 tbsp paprika", "1 tbsp sweet smoked paprika", "1 tbsp ground cumin", "0.5 tsp cayenne", "1 tsp ground cinnamon", "1 tsp dried oregano", "0.5 tsp dried chili flakes", "500 ml vegetable stock", "400 g borlotti beans (drained and rinsed)", "400 g cannellini beans (drained and rinsed)", "700 g sweet potato (peeled and cubed)", "800 g tinned tomatoes", "Salt and pepper", "2 limes"

Instructions

    "Cook onions and garlic in oil for 5 minutes.", "Add spices, sugar, and seasoning. Cook for 15-20 minutes.", "Mix in sweet potato, beans, and tomatoes. Cook for 45-60 minutes.", "Mash some beans for texture and serve."
