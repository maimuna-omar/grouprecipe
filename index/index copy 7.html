Recipe Ingredients

Soft and Light Cheese Cake

Ingredients

    "1 whole pie crust", "120 ml lemon juice", "150 g sugar", "2 tbsp cornstarch", "4 egg yolks", "4 egg whites", "30 g butter", "1 tsp cream of tartar"

Instructions

    "Preheat oven to 350℉(175℃). Bake pie crust until golden brown.", "Whisk lemon juice, sugar, and cornstarch in a pot until thickened.", "Add egg yolks and butter, cook until smooth.", "Whisk egg whites with cream of tartar, then fold into the lemon mixture.", "Pour filling into baked crust, bake for 10 minutes.", "Cool and serve."
