Recipe Ingredients

Pork and Prawn Egg Rolls

Ingredients

    "1kg (2.2 lbs) beef brisket, cut into 5cm cubes", "2 dried Ancho chilies", "1 dried Pasilla chili", "1 dried Chipotle chili", "4 cloves garlic, peeled", "½ onion", "½ tbsp oregano", "1 cinnamon stick", "1 clove", "40ml white vinegar", "Salsa verde, corn tortillas, diced onion, and radish for serving"

Instructions

    "Season brisket with salt. Sear in a pot until browned.", "Soak chilies, then blend with garlic, onion, oregano, cinnamon, clove, and vinegar.", "Cook beef with sauce in a slow cooker for 12 hours on low.", "Shred beef and serve in tortillas with salsa verde and toppings."
