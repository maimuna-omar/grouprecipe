Classic Margarita

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lime (cut into wedges)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup soda water
  • Crushed ice

Instructions

  1. Muddle mint leaves, lime wedges, and sugar in a glass.
  2. Fill with crushed ice, pour soda water, and stir.
  3. Garnish with a mint leaf.
