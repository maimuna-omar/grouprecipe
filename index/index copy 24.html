Red Lobster Shrimp

Ingredients

    "1 pound jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined", "2 tablespoons pinot grigio", "1 tablespoon melted butter", "2 teaspoons freshly squeezed lemon juice", "2 cloves garlic, minced", "1 pinch salt", "1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes", "2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese", "Chopped parsley for garnish", "Lemon wedges for garnish"

Instructions

    "Preheat the broiler to 500°F (260°C).", "Arrange shrimp in an oven-safe dish and add pinot grigio, butter, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and red pepper.", "Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and broil for 7 minutes.", "Garnish with parsley and serve with lemon wedges."
