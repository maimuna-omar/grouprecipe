Matcha Soft Cake

Ingredients

    "200 g dark chocolate", "100 g butter", "4 eggs", "100 g sugar", "50 g flour", "5 g vanilla extract"

Instructions

    "Preheat oven to 425℉(220℃). Butter and flour ramekins.", "Melt chocolate and butter together, stirring until smooth.", "Whisk eggs and sugar until thickened, then add melted chocolate and vanilla extract.", "Fold in flour until combined.", "Pour the batter into prepared ramekins, filling halfway.", "Bake for 12 minutes until edges are set but the center is soft.", "Allow to cool for a minute, then invert onto plates and serve warm."
