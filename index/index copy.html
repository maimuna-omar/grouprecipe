Cute Cow Roll Cake

Ingredients

    "80 g coconut milk", "60 g cake flour", "45 g vegetable oil", "45 g granulated sugar", "20 g cornstarch", "6 eggs", "1 g salt", "8 g vanilla extract", "8 g lemon juice", "black food coloring powder", "160 g heavy cream", "60 g cream cheese", "15 g granulated sugar (for filling)"

Instructions

    "Blend cucumber and lemon juice, strain into a pitcher, add honey, and mix well.", "Separate eggs; keep egg whites cool in the fridge.", "Mix oil, milk, sifted flour, cornstarch, and salt. Add 1 egg white and combine.", "Preheat oven to 302℉(150℃). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.", "Whip egg whites with lemon juice, sugar, and vanilla until soft peaks form. Fold into batter.", "Reserve a portion of batter, mix with black food coloring, and pipe cow patterns onto baking mat. Bake for 2 minutes.", "Pour the remaining batter onto the tray and bake for 20 minutes. Cool on a rack.", "For filling, whip cream cheese and sugar until smooth, then whip with heavy cream to stiff peaks.", "Spread filling on cake, roll, and chill for 1 hour before slicing."
