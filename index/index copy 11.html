Recipe Ingredients

Vegetarian Cottage Pie

Ingredients

    "3 tsp olive oil", "1.5 onions (finely chopped)", "4.5 cloves garlic (peeled and crushed)", "4.5 carrots (finely chopped)", "4.5 celery sticks (finely chopped)", "375 g chestnut mushrooms (finely chopped)", "3 tbsp flour", "1.5 tsp dried thyme", "1.5 tsp dried rosemary", "0.75 tsp freshly ground black pepper", "1200 g green lentils (drained and rinsed)", "6 tbsp tomato purée", "1125 ml vegetable stock", "3 tbsp Marmite", "3 tbsp Henderson's Relish", "1.5 kg white potatoes", "3 tbsp butter", "Salt and pepper"

Instructions

    "Heat oil in a pan and fry onions, garlic, carrots, and celery for 5 minutes.", "Add mushrooms and cook for another 5 minutes.", "Mix in flour, thyme, rosemary, and pepper. Cook for 3-5 minutes.", "Add lentils, purée, stock, Marmite, and Henderson's Relish. Cook for 30 minutes.", "Boil potatoes, mash with butter, salt, and pepper.", "Assemble with mash on top and bake at 200°C for 25 minutes."
