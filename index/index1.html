Banana Chiffon Cake

Ingredients

    "6 ripe bananas", "216 g vegetable oil", "120 g sugar", "300 g cake flour", "24 eggs", "6 tbsp lemon juice", "6 tbsp vanilla extract"

Instructions

    "Preheat oven to 302℉(150℃). Wrap the bottom of a 6-inch round chiffon cake pan with tin foil.", "Separate the eggs; keep the egg whites cool in the fridge.", "Mix mashed banana and vegetable oil, sift in cake flour, mix until smooth, then add 4 egg yolks and set aside.", "Whip egg whites with lemon juice, sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form. Gently fold in the batter.", "Pour batter into the cake pan, smooth the surface, and tap to release air pockets.", "Place the cake pan in a baking tray with hot water. Bake for 60 minutes or until golden brown.", "Cool the cake upside down on a rack, then loosen sides with a spatula to release.", "Dust with powdered sugar and serve."
