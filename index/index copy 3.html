Recipe Ingredients

Tiramisu

Ingredients

    "200 g ladyfinger cookies", "250 g mascarpone cheese", "200 g heavy cream", "200 g espresso", "50 g dark chocolate", "50 g sugar", "10 g cocoa powder", "5 g vanilla extract"

Instructions

    "Whip mascarpone with sugar and vanilla extract until smooth.", "Whip heavy cream to soft peaks, then fold into mascarpone mixture.", "Dip ladyfinger cookies in espresso, layer them at the bottom of a dish.", "Spread half the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.", "Add another layer of soaked ladyfingers, then top with the remaining mascarpone mixture.", "Chill in the refrigerator for 4 hours.", "Before serving, sprinkle with cocoa powder and grated dark chocolate."
