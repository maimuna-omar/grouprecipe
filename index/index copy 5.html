Mango Crepe Roll

Ingredients

    "250 g flour", "100 g sugar", "1 tbsp baking powder", "2 eggs", "150 ml milk", "75 g butter", "150 g blueberries", "5 g vanilla extract"

Instructions

    "Preheat oven to 375℉(190℃). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.", "Whisk flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.", "In another bowl, whisk eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.", "Fold wet ingredients into dry ingredients until just combined.", "Gently fold in blueberries.", "Spoon batter into muffin tins and bake for 20-25 minutes.", "Cool on a rack before serving."
