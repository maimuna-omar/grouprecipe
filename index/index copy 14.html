Recipe Ingredients

Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

    "1.5 tbsp olive oil", "375 g quinoa", "1125 ml vegetable stock", "6 courgettes (sliced lengthways)", "450 g asparagus", "375 g petit pois", "3 lemons (zest and juice)", "9 tbsp olive oil", "3 tbsp honey", "Fresh mint (chopped)", "Salt and pepper", "Parmesan"

Instructions

    "Cook quinoa in stock.", "Griddle courgettes and asparagus.", "Prepare dressing and combine all ingredients.", "Serve with Parmesan shavings."
