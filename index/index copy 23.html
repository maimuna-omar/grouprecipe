Recipe Ingredients

Mutton Goat Curry

Ingredients

    "2 pounds goat stew meat, cut into chunks", "2 teaspoons salt, divided", "1 teaspoon ground turmeric, divided", "½ teaspoon ground black pepper", "2 small red onions, quartered", "1 ½ tablespoons coconut oil", "1 cinnamon stick, broken into pieces", "2 whole star anise pods", "4 cardamom pods", "1 tablespoon fennel seeds", "½ teaspoon whole cloves", "2 tablespoons curry leaves, divided", "1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste", "1 small tomato, diced", "1 teaspoon coconut oil", "1 ½ teaspoons garam masala", "1 ½ teaspoons curry powder", "1 ½ teaspoons ground paprika", "½ cup chopped fresh cilantro"

Instructions

    "Season goat meat with salt, turmeric, and black pepper.", "Grind onions into a paste. Cook with spices in coconut oil until fragrant.", "Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and goat meat. Cook until browned.", "Transfer to a slow cooker and cook for 2-3 hours on High.", "Add spice mix, thicken gravy, and serve garnished with cilantro."
