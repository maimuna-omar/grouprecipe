Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

    "200 g flour", "100 g sugar", "75 g butter", "1 tbsp baking powder", "150 ml milk", "200 g fresh strawberries", "200 g whipped cream"

Instructions

    "Preheat oven to 375℉(190℃). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.", "Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.", "Cut in butter until the mixture resembles crumbs.", "Add milk and mix until just combined.", "Shape dough into rounds, bake for 15-20 minutes.", "Slice strawberries and whip cream.", "Assemble by layering shortcakes with whipped cream and strawberries."
